Across the country, many visually impaired voters are still struggling to cast their ballots.

Disenfranchised Voters in Montana

Montana is home to 143,500 disenfranchised voters with disabilities, with 29,300 being visually impaired. The Electronic Ballot Request System (EBRS) was only used by seven individuals who had requested it. The system has been reportedly been glitching when used by voters.

PA Mail-In Ballots Unaccessible For Disabled Voters

According to the Courier Times, 1.7 million Pennsylvanians (14% of the state) has physical, hearing, or visual disability and they are more common in rural populations where the older population lives. 26 of the 150,000 of visually impaired voters applied for the “accessible write in ballot” which was developed after the NFB sued.
