Can’t See, Can’t Vote

This 2020 election is the most important election in our lifetime. Because of COVID-19, for the first time, many states will have allowed universal mail-in ballots to be sent to every household regardless of application. They send them without considering whether or not the homeowner is visually impaired. Individuals who prefer to vote in person find themselves facing environmental barriers, untrained staff, and machines not working. The Help America Vote Act (HAVA) was passed in 2002 by Congress and President Bush in order to improve voting accessibility to people with disabilities. We need to make sure it is being enforced and full suffrage is acheived.
